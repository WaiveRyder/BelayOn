    About

    What is BelayOn?

    To put it simply, BelayOn is a member management tool designed for the modern climbing gym.

    Purpose

    BelayOn's purpose is to help staff keep track of their members and guests efficiently, allowing guests to spend more time climbing and staff more time with guests.

    Benefits

    By increasing the speed of guest and member checkins not only do guests get maximum time enjoying the gym experience, but staff has more time for safety, orientations, and membership conversions.
